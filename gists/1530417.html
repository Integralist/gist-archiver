Dimensions.md

https://developer.mozilla.org/en/Determining_the_dimensions_of_elements

Get dimensions that include everything except margin space (so borders + padding): offsetWidth and offsetHeight

Get dimensions for just content: clientWidth and clientHeight

Get dimensions of total height (regardless if any part is clipped/hidden): scrollWidth and scrollHeight
